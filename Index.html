Latest Sports News

Football Team Heads to National Finals

SRM's football team continues their remarkable journey as they head to the National Finals after a nail-biting 2-1 victory in the semi-finals. Captain Rahul Sharma scored the winning goal in the final minutes, driving the crowd into a frenzy. The final match is scheduled for September 25, 2024, at SRM Main Stadium.

Basketball League Champions: SRM Triumphs

SRM's basketball team has been crowned state champions after a resounding 88-74 victory in the final. Led by MVP Aditya Roy, the team dominated their opponents with aggressive offense and solid defense.

Kabaddi Team's Dominating Win in the Semifinals

SRM's kabaddi team secured a stunning victory in the semifinals with a 40-point lead. Captain Vivek Kumar led the charge, and the team's flawless raiding tactics helped seal their place in the finals on October 1, 2024.

Volleyball Team Prepares for Regional Tournament

SRM's volleyball team is preparing for the regional tournament on November 8, 2024. Coach Neha Verma is optimistic about the team's chances after a series of strong performances this season.
